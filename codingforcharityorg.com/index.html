Helping The World, One Website At A Time



Through websites, we can provide businesses with a platform for online ordering/scheduling, pickups, showcasing services, and providing information to their customers.


About

When COVID-19 hit, we began to realize the impacts it was having on our community. Small businesses were finding it hard to continue operating as usual due to the public wanting safer options like curbside pickup for shopping due to rising safety concerns. However, small businesses lacked the funds to create such expensive websites with online ordering since it can cost thousands. Businesses were left to choose whether to close down their businesses temporarily, continue operating with the risk of possible contracting and spreading COVID, or having to conduct business through the cumbersome process of placing orders through phone calls.

Hence, we sought out to solve this problem. We are an organization of 33+ high schoolers and middle schoolers from all around the globe who utilize our coding skills to code websites with online ordering for businesses and even non-profits who have now started reaching out to us. To further aid the businesses as well as the rest of our community, we do not have a set charging price. Instead, in return for the websites, we ask for donations to non-profits like CRY (Child Rights and You), an organization bent on ensuring the basic rights of healthcare, education, food, and shelter is provided to unprivileged children in India. And in the current situation with COVID-19, fundraising is more critical then ever. Already unprivileged children are finding themselves in continuously worsening conditions and non-profits like CRY are having to take extra steps to ensure they can reach as many children as safely as possible.


Our Mission

Our mission is to provide local businesses with a technological platform to safely interact with customers during the current COVID-19 outbreak as well as fundraising money for COVID-19 aid.
