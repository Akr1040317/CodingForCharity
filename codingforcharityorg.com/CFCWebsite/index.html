CodingForCharity

Helping The World, One Website At A Time



Through websites, we can provide businesses with a platform for online ordering/scheduling, pickups, showcasing services, and providing information to their customers.


About

We are an experienced group of student coders located in the Sanford/Lake Mary area looking to help out the community during the current COVID-19 outbreak by developing websites.

During the current outbreak, many local businesses have been suffering from a drastic loss in customers. Companies like you are turning towards online websites where viewers can access information about their companies, place order for pickups, or view updates during the pandemic.

We are willing to create, design, and implement such a website for local businesses at a respectably cheap price. All profits go towards non-profits related to Covid-19 relief.


Our Mission

Our mission is to provide local businesses with a technological platform to safely interact with customers during the current COVID-19 outbreak as well as fundraising money for COVID-19 aid.
